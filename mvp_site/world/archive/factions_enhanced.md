## Factions

### The Celestial Imperium
The surviving remnant of the original divine order, struggling to maintain control over a fractured realm through rigid adherence to ancient laws and celestial bloodline supremacy.

**Leadership Structure:**
• Supreme Authority: The absent Creator (divine mandate maintained)
• Lord Regent: Raziel, First Scion with Starlit Eyes variant - rules from Empyrean Citadel for over a millennium
• Imperial Hierarchy: Prince Artorius (heir apparent, deceased Year 85), Empyrean nobles with Unseeing Eyes
• Military Command: Prefects commanding regiments, Centurions leading companies, specialized commanders
• Administrative: Magisters like Lucian Kantos (Keeper of Hallowed Archives with 10,000+ years recorded history)

**Military Capabilities:**
• Professional Legions: Highly disciplined armies with standardized equipment and formations
• Elite Units: Silent Guard (white masks, bone armor, light wings - Raziel's personal retinue), Starfall Guard (elite formations)
• Magical Assets: Celestial beings manifesting wings of light, advanced scrying through Chamber of Echoes
• Fortifications: Massive complexes like Fortress Vigil, Western Wall, Seraph's Watch
• Tactics: Disciplined shield walls, coordinated formations, surgical strikes, defensive mastery

**Sub-factions:**
• **The Principate Senate**: Traditionalist power base of oldest families - Political intrigue masters managing Empyrean Affliction - Led by Raziel's guidance. Includes progressive Revisionist Scholars wing questioning official doctrine under Magister Lucian Kantos.
• **The Starfall Command**: Military faction around Alexiel's legacy - Believes in martial strength over deliberation - Led by twin princes Cassian and Darius
• **House Sanguis**: Arch-traditionalists opposing reform - Led by Lord Vorlag, represents bloodline purists

### The Shattered Host (Formerly Unchained Host)
Scattered remnants of Lucifer's rebellion, broken into competing factions after the Starfall Gambit but united in opposition to the Imperium.

**Former Leadership Structure (When Unified):**
• Supreme Commander: Lucifer (The Morning Star) - operated from Obsidian Spire until defeat
• Seven Apostates: Original commanders with reality-altering powers, including Lord Marshal Xylos
• Regional Commanders: Like Alexiel (former "Prodigal Sword" before defection)
• Warlords: Brutal commanders like Grull and Ghor'mar leading through strength
• Field Lieutenants: Local commanders like Gorok implementing efficiency doctrine

**Former Military Capabilities:**
• Numbered Legions: Seventh Fang Legion under Xylos, others under various Apostates
• Mixed Forces: Orcs, humans, beast-kin, other non-human races united against oppression
• Elite Units: Apostate Guard with crimson wings, Shadow Stalkers for tracking, Iron-Tusk Crushers
• Special Assets: Gnomish alchemy for siege warfare, psychic communication networks
• Tactics: Overwhelming force, systematic purging, terror warfare, alchemical demolitions

**Current Sub-factions (Post-Fragmentation):**
• **The Obsidian Covenant**: Organized successor-state with sophisticated philosophy - Attracts nobles/mages/intellectuals - Led by Malakor the Argent Tongue
• **The Carrion Court**: Nomadic war-band following law of the strong - Pure chaos force constantly raiding - Led by Griselda the Carrion Queen
• **The Unchained Mind**: Fanatical zealots dedicated to pure anti-authoritarian philosophy - Operates in secret cells spreading revolution - Led by Perrin the Apostate Scholar

### The Seven Apostates (Lucifer's Generals)
**Original Roster:** Lucifer (Morning Star - defeated), Samael (Unbeliever), Abaddon (Unchained Fury), Lord Marshal Xylos (Crimson Flame), Leliel (Whispering Prince), Uriel (Broken Wall), Azrael (First Sword)

**Key Details:**
• Lord Marshal Xylos: Commands Apostate Guard with crimson wings, sophisticated tactician who recognized Alexiel's deeper game
• Each Apostate possesses Tier 6-7 reality-altering powers
• Following Lucifer's defeat, some remain loyal while others pursue independent agendas
• Command structure fragmented but individual power remains formidable

### The First-Spear Clans
Former slaves forging independent nation through warrior tradition, struggling with transition from revolutionary movement to stable governance.

**Sub-factions:**
• **The High Council**: Established leadership focused on nation-building - Pragmatic approach to diplomacy and stability - Led by War-Chief Vorlag
• **The Tusk-Sworn**: Young aggressive warriors believing Clans grown soft - Advocates eternal war and constant raids - Led by Karg the Iron-Tusk
• **The Spirit-Walkers**: Shamanistic heart concerned with spirit world - Provides third perspective between caution and recklessness - Led by goblin shaman Zanja

### The Tsuran Dominion
Honor-bound feudal society maintaining strict neutrality while pursuing internal power struggles according to rigid codes.

**Sub-factions:**
• **The Akai Concord**: Traditionalist faction representing founding principles - Isolationist and xenophobic honor code adherents - Led by Daimyo Kenjiro
• **The Shiro Alliance**: Progressive mercantile faction pushing boundaries - Believes engagement and trade necessary for survival - Led by Lady Asami
• **The Shroud-bearers**: Secret police whose existence is officially denied - Eliminates threats to Dominion stability - Led by The Ghost
