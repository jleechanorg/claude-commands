## Geography & Locations

### Major Powers & Territory Control
**Celestial Imperium**: ~25% of world, eastern regions centered on Aeterna
**Shattered Host Remnants**: ~25% of world in scattered western territories  
**Independent Powers**: ~50% including Tsuran Dominion, Adamant Vault, First-Spear Clans, Nexus

### Imperial Cities & Strongholds
**Aeterna** (Celestial Imperium) - Capital built with impossible geometry as magical anchor enforcing divine laws; houses Zenith Spire containing royal family
**Zenith Spire** (Celestial Imperium) - Raziel's personal seat of power and Imperial command center within Aeterna
**Empyrean Citadel** (Celestial Imperium) - Lord Regent Raziel's primary residence containing Chamber of Echoes with 10,000 years of recorded history
**Seraph's Watch** (Celestial Imperium/Contested) - Pivotal mountain fortress controlling strategic pass, frequently changes hands
**The Western Wall** (Celestial Imperium) - Critical defensive fortification where Alexiel earned heroic reputation
**Fortress Vigil** (Celestial Imperium) - Major defensive position in Contested Territories where Alexiel proved tactical genius

### Former Host Territories
**Obsidian Spire** (Contested/Abandoned) - Lucifer's former fortress with advanced training facilities and strategic command center
**Silverwood** (Destroyed) - Town annihilated in Lucifer's campaign, catalyst for Alexiel's defection; now symbol of Host brutality
**Fort Sycorax** (Historical) - Early battle site where Alexiel learned "efficiency over sentiment" under Host doctrine

### Strategic Battle Sites
**The Sacrifice Fields** (Contested) - Where Alexiel died saving Sariel from warlord Mordan (Year 10)
**The Betrayer's Bridge** (Unknown) - Memorial site of Alexiel's death with ongoing political significance
**The Weeping Plains** (Neutral) - Centuries-long stalemate site marking expansion limits during Thousand Year War
**Contested Territories** (No Man's Land) - Wasteland between major powers with ruined settlements and magical contamination

### Geographic Features
**Mountain Passes** (Strategic) - Control access between major territories, frequently targeted by alchemical explosives
**Western Regions** (Host-Influenced) - Mixed-species settlements and industrial centers supporting former war machine
**Eastern Territories** (Imperial) - Traditional lands with established cities and rigid social hierarchies
**The Hallowed Archives** (Aeterna) - Repository of Imperial knowledge within inner sanctum walls

### Special Locations
**The Silent Throne** (Mythical) - Ultimate seat of power that cannot be claimed without risking reality collapse
**Chamber of Echoes** (Empyrean Citadel) - Magical recording facility storing 10,000 years of historical conversations
**Training Atrium** (Obsidian Spire) - Lucifer's private facility containing Abyssal Constructs for elite combat practice
