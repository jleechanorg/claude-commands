### Supporting Characters (From Alexiel Book)

#### Lord Marshal Xylos
**Faction:** Unchained Host
**Role:** Original Apostate, Military Commander
**Key Traits:** Sophisticated tactician, recognizes deeper games, commands crimson-winged Guard
**Background:** One of Lucifer's original Apostates, direct subordinate commanding major operations. Leads Seventh Fang Legion. Observed Siege of Vigil via scrying, realized Alexiel played deeper game.
**Abilities:** Reality-altering Apostate powers, strategic genius, crimson wings

#### Gorok
**Faction:** Unchained Host (Former)
**Role:** Beastman Lieutenant
**Key Traits:** Literal philosophy interpreter, brutal efficiency
**Background:** Led Silverwood massacre killing civilians including children. Hunted Alexiel with 12 elite trackers after her defection. Killed by Alexiel at waterfall battle.
**Significance:** His actions triggered Alexiel's moral awakening and defection

#### Warlord Grull
**Faction:** Unchained Host
**Role:** Berserker Commander
**Key Traits:** Hulking Orc, utterly devoted to Lucifer, raw combat power
**Background:** Major Host military commander, leads through strength and fear
**Abilities:** Tier 4-5 warrior, berserker rage, commands orc battalions

#### Warlord Ghor'mar
**Faction:** Unchained Host (Former)
**Role:** Orc Champion
**Key Traits:** Three-stroke combat pattern, overconfident
**Background:** Led assault on Fortress Vigil via gnomish alchemy blast. Killed by Alexiel who predicted his exact three-move sequence, proving her tactical genius.
**Death:** Surgical defeat demoralized Host forces, turned Alexiel from prisoner to hero

#### Prefect Quintus
**Faction:** Celestial Imperium
**Role:** Fortress Commander
**Key Traits:** Scarred veteran, follows orders precisely, professional soldier
**Background:** Commands Fortress Vigil, initially imprisoned Alexiel under extreme prejudice orders. Witnessed her transformation during siege defense.
**Abilities:** Veteran military commander, defensive tactics expert

#### Ser Bastion
**Faction:** Celestial Imperium
**Role:** Starfall Guard Veteran
**Key Traits:** "Solid as stone", reliable aide, trusted warrior
**Background:** Elite guard who served during Alexiel's rise, became trusted aide
**Abilities:** Elite combatant, celestial powers, unwavering loyalty

#### Veteran-Sergeant Cato
**Faction:** Celestial Imperium
**Role:** Vanguard Leader
**Key Traits:** Scarred officer, devoted to Alexiel, leads from front
**Background:** Leads vanguard forces, developed deep loyalty to Alexiel after witnessing her abilities
**Abilities:** Experienced combat leader, tactical expertise

#### Matriarch Tiberia Varrick
**Faction:** Celestial Imperium
**Role:** Noble House Leader
**Key Traits:** Sharp political operator, major house head, pragmatic
**Background:** Leads House Varrick, navigates Imperial politics with skill
**Influence:** Controls significant resources and political connections

#### Lord Vorlag
**Faction:** Celestial Imperium
**Role:** House Sanguis Leader
**Key Traits:** Arch-traditionalist, opposes Alexiel, bloodline purist
**Background:** Leads conservative faction opposing reforms and Alexiel's influence
**Opposition:** Represents old guard resistance to change

### Elite Units & Forces

#### Silent Guard
**Faction:** Celestial Imperium
**Description:** Elite guards with white masks, bone armor, manifest wings of light
**Role:** Raziel's personal retinue, supreme combat unit
**Capabilities:** Tier 5+ warriors, perfect coordination, absolute loyalty

#### Apostate Guard
**Faction:** Unchained Host
**Description:** Crimson-winged elite forces under Xylos command
**Role:** Lucifer's elite shock troops
**Capabilities:** Reality-warping powers, aerial supremacy

#### Third Imperial Regiment
**Faction:** Celestial Imperium
**Description:** Disgraced unit transformed into elite force under Alexiel
**Transformation:** From punishment duty to legendary status
**Morale:** Absolute devotion to Alexiel after redemption

#### Seventh Fang Legion
**Faction:** Unchained Host
**Description:** Major military force led by Lord Marshal Xylos
**Composition:** Mixed species army, heavy assault focus
**Reputation:** Fear and destruction follow their advance

#### Iron-Tusk Crushers
**Faction:** Unchained Host (Former)
**Description:** Berserker elite unit formerly under Ghor'mar
**Specialty:** Shock assault, breaking defensive lines
**Status:** Leaderless after Ghor'mar's death
