The Model Context Protocol (MCP) ecosystem has rapidly expanded since its late 2024 introduction by Anthropic, creating a diverse landscape of servers that bridge AI assistants like Claude with external tools, databases, and services. ## MCP Ecosystem Overview

### What is Model Context Protocol?
- **Open Standard**: Universal protocol for connecting AI systems with data sources
- **Two-Way Communication**: Secure connections between AI assistants and external systems
- **Unified Integration**: Replaces fragmented point-to-point integrations
- **Claude Native**: Built-in support in Claude Desktop with expanding client ecosystem

### Ecosystem Growth
- **Rapid Adoption**: Explosive growth since late 2024 launch
- **Community Driven**: Strong community contribution and development
- **Enterprise Ready**: Major companies building official integrations
- **Diverse Applications**: Servers spanning development, productivity, enterprise, and specialized domains
