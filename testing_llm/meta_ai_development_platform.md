**Purpose**: Systematize entire development process with AI partnership

**Key Features**:

- **Decision Automation Framework**: Routine decisions automated, complex decisions escalated
- **Continuous Learning System**: Tracks decision outcomes to improve future choices

**Efficiency Multipliers**:
- **Code Review**: LLM partnership in all development decisions
- **Architecture Support**: AI evaluates technical alternatives with reasoning
- **Library Selection**: Automated selection of appropriate tools and packages
- **Quality Prediction**: Learn which decisions lead to successful outcomes

**Key Features**:
