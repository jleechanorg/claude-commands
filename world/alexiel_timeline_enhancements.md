# Timeline Enhancements for Alexiel Book

This document contains all the timeline enhancements to be applied to the Alexiel book.

## 1. Arc Timeline Summaries

### Arc 1 Summary (to be added at beginning of Arc 1)
```
### Timeline Summary: Pre-Arc 1
- Year 61: Alexiel is created by Lucifer
- Year 71: At age 10, begins advanced combat training  
- Year 74: At age 13, masters combat against constructs
- Year 76: At age 15, receives "Promise of the Throne"
- Year 78: Current story begins, Alexiel age 17
```

### Arc 2 Summary (to be added between Arc 1 and Arc 2)
```
### Timeline Summary: Arc 1 → Arc 2
- Year 78, Month of Scourge: Alexiel defects at age 17
- Year 78, Month of Scourge: Arrives at Fortress Vigil
- Year 78, Month of Mirtul: Travels to Aeterna
- Year 78, Month of Eleasis: Marries Artorius (age 17)
- Year 78, Month of Eleint: Takes command of Third Regiment
- Year 79: Birth of twins Cassian and Valerius (Alexiel age 18)
- Year 80: Birth of daughter Sariel (Alexiel age 19)
```

### Arc 3 Summary (to be added later in story)
```
### Timeline Summary: Arc 2 → Arc 3
- Year 78: Alexiel defects and marries at age 17
- Year 79-81: Consolidates power in the Imperium
- Year 82: Assassination attempt at age 21
- Year 82: Alexiel appointed as Magister of the Watch
- Year 83: Battle of Mourning Fields at age 22
```

## 2. Age Markers to Add

After timestamps at key moments:
- Year 78 events: `[Alexiel: Age 17]`
- Year 79 events: `[Alexiel: Age 18]`
- Year 80 events: `[Alexiel: Age 19]`
- Year 82 events: `[Alexiel: Age 21]`
- Year 83 events: `[Alexiel: Age 22]`

## 3. "Years Since" References

For major time jumps:
- Year 82 scenes: `[4 years since defection / 3 years since marriage]`
- Year 83 scenes: `[5 years since defection / 4 years since marriage]`

## 4. Relative Time Markers

Before timestamps when jumping forward:
- `[Three weeks after the wedding...]`
- `[Four years later...]`
- `[One year later...]`
- `[Six months had passed since...]`

## 5. Game Stats to Replace

Replace all instances of:
- `[Key Game Stats: Alexiel - Level X...]` → Remove entirely
- `Level X` → Narrative descriptions like:
  - "her skills still developing" (low levels)
  - "a seasoned warrior" (mid levels)
  - "at the peak of her martial prowess" (high levels)
- `Fatigue: X` → `[Physical exhaustion weighing on her movements]`
- `Extra Attack (x2)` → `[Years of training evident in her fluid strikes]`

## 6. Seasonal/Cultural References

Add these throughout:
- "The Spring Equinox celebrations of Year 79 marked their first anniversary..."
- "As the Harvest Festival approached..."
- "The Winter Solstice found the family..."
- "During the Festival of Starfall..."

## 7. Children's Ages

Always specify when children appear:
- "Sariel, now four years old..."
- "The twins, seven and growing rapidly..."
- "Baby Sariel, only six months old..."
