## Rules & Restrictions

### Magical Constraints

**Power Limitations**
• Sustained magical use causes progressive fatigue and eventual collapse
• Wing manifestation indicates both power level and faction allegiance
• Nullification fields permanently disable magical items within range
• Psychic resonance devices can backfire if improperly calibrated
• Certain technologies require specific bloodlines or genetic markers

**Nullification Effects**
• Complete negation of active magical abilities within field range
• Permanent damage to magical items and artifacts
• Disruption of communication and scrying networks
• Psychological effects on magic-dependent beings
• Cannot be overcome by higher-tier magical power

### Genetic & Bloodline Restrictions

**Empyrean Affliction**
• Genetic curse affecting royal bloodlines with each generation
• Manifests as progressive paranoia, rage, and emotional instability
• Increases magical sensitivity while reducing mental stability
• Eventually leads to complete psychological breakdown
• Cannot be cured by conventional magical means

**Bloodline Hierarchies**
• Empyrean bloodlines possess "Unseeing Eyes" marking celestial heritage
• Power inheritance follows genetic lines but can skip generations
• Nulls appear randomly regardless of bloodline purity
• Hybrid bloodlines create unpredictable magical manifestations
• Social position directly correlates with bloodline purity

### Political & Social Constraints

**Imperial Limitations**
• Noble houses compete for influence while maintaining crown loyalty
• Rigid social hierarchies prevent rapid advancement
• Traditional precedent constrains policy flexibility
• International law limits actions against foreign powers
• Secret organizations pursue independent agendas

**Host Restrictions**
• Brutal tactics often backfire by creating stronger resistance
• Brittle command structure collapses when key officers eliminated
• Mixed-species armies have inherent cultural conflicts
• Efficiency doctrine can justify counterproductive actions
• Absolute loyalty requirements prevent tactical flexibility

### Strategic & Military Restrictions

**Territorial Limitations**
• Contested Territories increasingly uninhabitable from magical contamination
• Geographic barriers limit strategic movement and supply lines
• Resource scarcity forces difficult allocation decisions
• Weather patterns influenced by large-scale magical conflicts
• Natural creatures and phenomena pose ongoing operational threats

**Combat Restrictions**
• Tier differences create significant but not insurmountable disparities
• Tactical formations can overcome individual power advantages
• Environmental factors affect magical ability effectiveness
• Sustained combat depletes magical reserves rapidly
• Certain weapons considered too dangerous for regular use

### Cosmic & Metaphysical Rules

**The Silent Throne**
• Cannot be claimed without risking reality collapse into pure order or chaos
• Represents ultimate cosmic balance that must be maintained
• Both factions seek it while being unable to possess it
• Attempting to claim triggers universal defensive mechanisms
• Serves as eternal war's ultimate unattainable prize

**Universal Balance**
• Order and chaos must remain in dynamic equilibrium
• Extreme actions trigger automatic balancing responses
• The eternal war serves as mechanism preserving balance
• Divine intervention occurs only to prevent complete imbalance
• Mortal actions influence but cannot override cosmic principles
