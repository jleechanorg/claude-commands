## World History & Timeline

### Age of Dominion (Ancient Era)
**Creation Era** - Divine order established, cosmic balance designed between order and chaos
**The Forging of Assiah** - Creator and Seraphim conquer primordial chaos world, establish divine order
**The Sundering of Primordials** - Cosmic war against elemental beings shatters largest landmass
**The First City of Aeterna** - Built as continent-spanning magical ritual to enforce divine laws
**The Edict of Chains** - All "chaotic" races (elves, dwarves, orcs, goblins, humans) enslaved
**The Thousand Year War** - Campaign against Draconic-Abyssal Concord establishes divine dominance
**The Great Vanishing** - Creator and Seraphim depart without explanation, leave Empyrean Bloodline to rule

### Age of Rebellion (The Twin Stars Era - Year 0)
**Rise of the Twin Stars** - Raziel and Lucifer emerge as greatest celestial champions
**Lucifer's Turn** - Discovery that Creator enjoyed eternal war and never intended victory
**Year 0: The Rebellion Begins** - Lucifer forms Unchained Host, eternal war starts
**March of Broken Chains** - Lucifer liberates slave races, building multi-species revolutionary army
**First Siege of Aeterna** - Host breaches two of three legendary walls before strategic retreat
**The Sundering of Ideals** - Raziel and Lucifer's final philosophical break over order vs. freedom

### Age of the Mortal Star (Alexiel's Era - Years 61-87)
**Year 61: Creation of Alexiel** - Lucifer creates ultimate weapon as perfect heir to rebellion
**Years 61-67: Early Training** - Alexiel begins combat training under Lucifer's direct guidance
**Year 67-76: Advanced Development** - Develops legendary tactical abilities and strategic mind
**Year 76: Battle of Fort Sycorax** - Alexiel learns "efficiency over sentiment" doctrine
**Years 76-78: Rise to Prominence** - Multiple Host victories establish Alexiel as greatest commander

#### The Silverwood Crisis (Year 78 - Detailed Breakdown)
**Day 1 Morning** - Silverwood falls to Host forces in under 1 hour via Alexiel's strategy
**Day 1 Afternoon** - Silverwood Massacre: Lt. Gorok kills civilians including children
**Day 1 Evening** - Alexiel's moral awakening: witnesses dead children, realizes Host's true nature
**Day 1 Night** - Alexiel defects from Host: drops sigil, flees into wilderness

**Days 2-4: The Hunt** - Gorok's party pursues; Alexiel kills all 12 trackers at waterfall
**Day 5: Imperial Contact** - Prince Artorius at Vigil; Alexiel captured by Imperial patrol
**Days 6-19: Interrogations** - Raziel suspects trap; Alexiel reveals Host tactics
**Day 20: Siege of Vigil** - Alexiel granted command, kills Warlord Ghor'mar, becomes hero
**Days 22-35: March to Seraph's Watch** - Journey and final battle revealing true nature

#### Consolidation Period (Years 79-87)
**Year 79: Union of Hope** - Marriage to Prince Artorius creates new political alliance
**Year 80: Birth of Cassian** - First heir born as symbol of unity
**Year 82: Birth of Twins** - Darius and Sariel born; assassination attempt reveals Alexiel's nature
**Year 85: Battle of Mourning Fields** - Lucifer kills Artorius in personal combat
**Year 87: The Starfall Gambit** - Alexiel leads 30 elite guards against Lucifer and Six Apostates
**Year 87: Victory and Loss** - Only Alexiel survives, Lucifer defeated but Host fragments

### Age of Splintered Crowns (Modern Era - Year 88+)
**Year 88: Treaty of Nexus** - Formal end of bipolar power structure, multi-polar world emerges
**Years 88-98: The New Peace** - Uneasy stability as various factions consolidate power
**Year 10 New Peace: Battle of Sacrifice Fields** - Alexiel dies saving Sariel from warlord Mordan
**Year 11 New Peace, Day 1: Anniversary of Loss** - First anniversary of Alexiel's death
**Year 11 New Peace, Day 2: Campaign Beginning** - Current timeline starts, new generation takes power

### Key Turning Points
• **Silverwood Massacre** - Triggers Alexiel's defection and fundamental shift in war dynamics
• **Siege of Vigil** - Proves Alexiel's tactical genius and loyalty to new cause
• **Battle of Seraph's Watch** - Reveals Alexiel's true supernatural nature to world
• **Assassination Attempt (Year 82)** - Forces broader acknowledgment of new reality
• **The Starfall Gambit** - Ends Lucifer's reign but fragments Host into warlord states
• **Battle of Sacrifice Fields** - Alexiel's death marks end of an era

### Strategic Evolution Through Ages
• **Ancient Era** - Divine order imposed through overwhelming power
• **Rebellion Era** - Conventional warfare and mass liberation movements
• **Alexiel Era** - Individual excellence and tactical innovation dominate
• **Modern Era** - Fragmented powers and political maneuvering replace total war
