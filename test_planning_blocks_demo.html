Planning Block Buttons Demo

This demonstrates how planning block choices are rendered as clickable buttons.

Example Story Content

Scene #1: The goblin chieftain snarls at your approach, his yellowed tusks gleaming in the torchlight. His warriors shift nervously, hands on their crude weapons.

What do you do?

Click Output:

Click a button above to see what text would be sent to the LLM...

How it works:

  • Planning block choices are automatically detected using regex pattern: **[ActionWord_Number]:** Description
  • Each choice is rendered as a styled button with hover effects
  • Clicking a button sends the full choice text (e.g., "Action_1: Draw your sword...")
  • Buttons are disabled during interaction and re-enabled after response
  • Special characters in choice text are properly escaped
