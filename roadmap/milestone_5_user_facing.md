Based on typical RPG platform usage, here are the user-facing features that would provide immediate value:

## **Step 5.1: Advanced Campaign Management (60 minutes)**

### **5.1.1 Campaign Organization System**
- **Campaign Folders/Tags**: Organize campaigns by setting, genre, or group
- **Favorites System**: Star important campaigns for quick access
- **Campaign Status**: Active, Completed, On Hold, Archived
- **Quick Actions**: Duplicate campaign, mark as template, bulk operations

### **5.1.2 Enhanced Campaign Cards**
- **Campaign Thumbnails**: Auto-generated or custom images for each campaign
- **Progress Indicators**: Session count, story progression, character levels
- **Recent Activity**: Last action taken, next session reminder
- **Campaign Statistics**: Total play time, sessions completed, choices made

### **5.1.3 Advanced Search & Filtering**
- **Filter by Status**: Active, completed, archived campaigns
- **Filter by Genre**: Fantasy, sci-fi, horror, custom tags
- **Filter by Date**: Recently played, created this week/month
- **Sort Options**: Alphabetical, last played, creation date, session count

---

## **Step 5.2: Session History & Save Points (45 minutes)**

### **5.2.1 Session Timeline**
- **Session Browser**: Navigate through all past game sessions
- **Key Moments**: Bookmark important story beats and decisions
- **Session Summaries**: AI-generated recap of each session
- **Story Branches**: Visual representation of major decision points

### **5.2.2 Save Point System**
- **Manual Save Points**: Save at critical moments before big decisions
- **Auto-Save Points**: Automatic saves at chapter transitions
- **Save Point Names**: Custom names for easy identification
- **Load Previous State**: Roll back to any previous save point

### **5.2.3 Character & World State Tracking**
- **Character Development**: Track stats, abilities, equipment over time
- **Relationship Tracker**: NPC relationships and faction standings
- **World Changes**: How player decisions affected the world state
- **Story Consistency**: Ensure narrative continuity across sessions

---

## **Step 5.3: Mobile-First User Experience (45 minutes)**

### **5.3.1 Touch-Optimized Interface**
- **Swipe Gestures**: Swipe between campaigns, navigate history
- **Touch Targets**: Larger buttons and tap areas for mobile
- **Mobile Navigation**: Collapsible sidebar, bottom navigation
- **Quick Actions**: Long-press context menus, pull-to-refresh

### **5.3.2 Offline Reading Mode**
- **Download Sessions**: Save campaign sessions for offline reading
- **Offline Story Browser**: Read past sessions without internet
- **Sync on Reconnect**: Automatically sync when connection restored
- **Mobile Notifications**: Session reminders, campaign updates

### **5.3.3 Mobile-Specific Features**
- **Voice Input**: Speak your responses instead of typing
- **Camera Integration**: Take photos for campaign inspiration
- **Location Integration**: Save where you played each session
- **Share Features**: Easy sharing of epic moments to social media

---

## **Step 5.4: Personalization & Accessibility (30 minutes)**

### **5.4.1 User Preferences**
- **Reading Preferences**: Font size, line spacing, paragraph width
- **Interface Density**: Compact, comfortable, spacious layouts
- **Color Accessibility**: High contrast mode, colorblind-friendly palettes
- **Reading Speed**: Adjust text reveal speed for dramatic effect

### **5.4.2 Custom Themes & Layouts**
- **Theme Builder**: Create custom color schemes beyond the 4 defaults
- **Layout Options**: Card view, list view, magazine layout
- **Background Options**: Subtle textures, animated backgrounds
- **Font Choices**: Fantasy fonts, dyslexia-friendly fonts, serif options

### **5.4.3 Onboarding & Discovery**
- **Welcome Tour**: Interactive tutorial for new users
- **Feature Tooltips**: Discover advanced features gradually
- **Sample Campaign**: Pre-built campaign to explore features
- **Tips & Tricks**: Contextual tips based on usage patterns

---

## **Step 5.5: Content Sharing & Export (30 minutes)**

### **5.5.1 Campaign Sharing**
- **Share Links**: Generate shareable links to read-only campaigns
- **Campaign Templates**: Save campaigns as reusable templates
- **Export Options**: PDF adventure logs, EPUB story books
- **Social Features**: Share epic moments, character achievements

### **5.5.2 Import/Export System**
- **Backup All Data**: Complete account backup and restore
- **Campaign Transfer**: Move campaigns between accounts
- **Platform Migration**: Import from other RPG platforms
- **Format Support**: JSON, XML, plain text formats
